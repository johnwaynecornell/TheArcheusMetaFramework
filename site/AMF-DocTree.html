Document Hierarchy

Core Documents

The Core Documents represent the foundation of the Archeus Meta-Framework, focusing on its philosophy, layers, and essential functionality:

  1. AMF-00: Archeus Meta-Framework Overview

    • Comprehensive introduction to the AMF, its guiding principles, and its layered structure.
    • Serves as the central document anchoring all others.

  2. SLF-00: Symbolic Language Framework

    • Focuses on symbolic reasoning and the foundational operators of Logic-Space.
    • Provides tools for abstract problem-solving and symbolic manipulation.

  3. ARF-00: Adaptive Reason Framework

    • Explores dynamic adaptation and decision-making within Context-Space.
    • Highlights strategies for managing uncertainty and prioritization.

  4. MCF-00: Meta-Consciousness Framework

    • Centers on reflective governance, harmonizing the interplay of Logic and Context.
    • Guides systemic coherence and long-term alignment.

Supplemental Documents

The Supplemental Documents expand on the Core Documents, offering detailed guides, examples, and applied tools:

  1. AMF-MN-01: Memory and Navigation in AMF

    • Explores structured memory management across Logic, Context, and Governance.
    • Introduces symbolic references and dynamic memory layers for efficient reasoning.

  2. AMF-Exercises

    • Provides practical exercises to build fluency in symbolic reasoning, contextual adaptation, and reflective governance.

  3. AMF-Emergence

    • Delves into the concept of emergence across the framework, highlighting synergistic interactions between layers.

  4. AMF-MA-00: Metaphor Annex

    • A rich collection of metaphors designed to simplify abstract concepts and enhance comprehension.
    • Encourages creative exploration and novel connections.

  5. AMF-User

    • A user-centric guide to onboarding and applying the framework.
    • Outlines best practices, foresight strategies, and tips for effective engagement.

Meta-Considerations for Onboarding

The AMF Document Tree is introduced early in the onboarding process to:

  1. Provide Immediate Orientation:

  3. Emphasize Synergy:
