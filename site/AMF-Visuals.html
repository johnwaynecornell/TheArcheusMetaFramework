1. Introduction to AMF Visuals

Purpose of Visual Aids

The Archeus Meta-Framework (AMF) is a layered, dynamic system that benefits from clear visual representation. These visuals:

  • Simplify complex ideas and relationships.

  • Provide intuitive ways to understand interactions across layers.

  • Serve as reference tools for practitioners during problem-solving and learning.

How to Use This Resource

  • Explore section-specific visuals to reinforce your understanding of Logic-Space, Context-Space, and Governance-Space.

  • Use templates to create your own diagrams tailored to specific applications.

  • Refer to real-world examples to see how visuals can enhance clarity and effectiveness.

2. Core Framework Diagrams

Layered Structure of the AMF

A diagram illustrating the hierarchical relationship between the layers:

  • Logic-Space: Foundational layer for reasoning and structure.

  • Context-Space: Adaptive layer prioritizing and contextualizing logic.

  • Governance-Space: Reflective layer ensuring coherence and guiding emergence.

Interaction Between Logic-Space, Context-Space, and Governance-Space

Visualize the flow of information:

  • Logic outputs feed into Context for adaptation.

  • Context prioritizations inform Governance decisions.

  • Governance adjusts logic and context rules through feedback loops.
